VocaLamp

This was a 9 week project for GIX
My roles: user researcher, product designer, software engineer

Problem:

Children need to learn new words while reading, but using a phone to look them up is distracting.

Process:

Throughout the quarter, my team conducted secondary research and user interviews to inform a proposed solution. We presented the status of the project at 3 milestones, ending with a working prototype and demonstration.

Result:

We built a smart study lamp that detects when a user points at a word, speaks the word and definition, and saves them to a parent app to review and keep track of progress.

Further Work:

Since the end of this class, we presented for the next cohort, entered into UW's CoMotion startup incubator and are (as of Jan 2023) conducting user testing for a CHI 2023 research paper.
