Spotify Wear

Context

side project

My role: product designer, UX designer, UX researcher

Problem

Media controls are a major feature of popular wearables, but standalone streaming player features are rudimentary at best.

Process

Throughout the quarter, my team conducted secondary research and user interviews to inform a proposed solution. We presented the status of the project at 3 milestones, ending with a working prototype and demonstration.

Result

I designed a smartwatch focused on Spotify streaming, leveraging Wear OS specific features.
